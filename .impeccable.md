## Design Context

### Users

Your Mascot Now is a public character-recipe library for designers, developers, founders, and content creators who want expressive black-and-white mascot artwork built around their own identity. Visitors should be able to quickly browse the first 80 scenes, search and filter the collection, copy a scene-specific prompt, use their own portrait as the identity reference in ChatGPT Images, inspect an individual piece, share its URL, and download the supplied original or SVG.

### Brand Personality

Direct, graphic, handmade, and precise. The interface should feel like a working character-recipe system: approachable enough to explore, disciplined enough to trust, and explicit about the path from photo to personalized mascot.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use crisp rectangular recipe cards, hard-edged controls, cool neutral surfaces, carbon black, and non-photo blue. Provide equally intentional light and dark modes, while keeping artwork wells white so monochrome SVG linework remains accurate. Avoid pill-heavy roundness, oversized display typography, cream editorial layouts, dark-neon technology aesthetics, glass effects, gradient text, and borderless contact-sheet cells. Typography should be compact, legible, and predominantly typographic rather than decorative.

### Design Principles

1. Treat catalog structure as useful information: every number, mark, and label should identify an illustration or category.
2. Keep the artwork dominant and use the "Photo → mascot" recipe strip as the signature system.
3. Make searching, filtering, prompt copying, theme switching, previewing, sharing, and downloading work without explanation.
4. Use motion for orientation and state changes, with an equivalent reduced-motion experience.
5. Preserve accessibility, responsive behavior, and image performance as part of the visual quality floor.
