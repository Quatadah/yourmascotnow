## Design Context

### Users

Your Mascot Now is a public illustration library for designers, developers, founders, and content creators who need expressive black-and-white mascot artwork. Visitors should be able to quickly browse the first 80 illustrations, search by concept, filter the collection, inspect an individual piece, share its URL, and download the supplied original.

### Brand Personality

Handmade, witty, and precise. The interface should feel like an illustrator's working archive: approachable enough to explore, disciplined enough to trust, and confident enough to let the drawings remain the focus.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use a light "proof desk" environment inspired by animation exposure sheets, non-photo-blue pencils, crop marks, numbered contact sheets, and grease-pencil corrections. Avoid generic cream editorial layouts, dark neon technology aesthetics, glass effects, gradient text, and repetitive card grids. Typography should combine a restrained hand-painted display face with highly legible reading and catalog faces.

### Design Principles

1. Treat catalog structure as useful information: every number, mark, and label should identify an illustration or category.
2. Keep the artwork dominant and spend visual boldness on one proof-sheet signature system.
3. Make searching, filtering, previewing, sharing, and downloading work without explanation.
4. Use motion for orientation and state changes, with an equivalent reduced-motion experience.
5. Preserve accessibility, responsive behavior, and image performance as part of the visual quality floor.
